Company Profile

Sunyukt Electrosolar & IT solutions Pvt Ltd is formed in 2015. We are MNRE (Ministry of Renewable Energy) Channel Partner with RESCO (Reg No : MNRE/CP/GCRT/C/5036) and also Open Category Registered Agency with MEDA ( Maharashtra Energy Development Agency ).

Our main focus is on :

  • Non-Conventional renewable Solar Energy implementation, Design, installation, Product Sales and After Sales Service.
  • End to End Solution in Information Technology.

Social Responsibility towards society is main motto to form the company. We have planned to focus and support initially in rural areas of Maharashtra, Andra Pradesh & Karnataka where Electricity is challenge in day to day life and hence decided to facilitate our efforts to rural parts of Maharashtra, Andra Pradesh & Karnataka.
